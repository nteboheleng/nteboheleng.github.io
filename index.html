SELF-CONFIDENCE



Go confidently in the direction of your dream!Live the live you've imagined and remember,you only fail when you stop trying.Work for CAUSE not for APPLAUSE,live life to EXPRESS not to IMPRESS.It doesn't matter where you are from but only where you are going can determine where you are from.
